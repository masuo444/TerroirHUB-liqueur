Japanese Liqueur Food Pairing

Umeshu + Japanese Cuisine

Umeshu soda pairs beautifully with tempura, grilled fish, and vinegared dishes — its acidity cuts through oil and refreshes the palate. On the rocks complements cheese, nuts, and dried fruit. Straight umeshu is ideal for dessert time.

Yuzushu + Seafood

Yuzushu's refreshing citrus character is a natural match for sashimi, sushi, and carpaccio. The yuzu acidity works much like ponzu, complementing raw fish beautifully. Also an excellent companion to hot-pot dishes.

Momoshu (Peach) + Desserts

The sweet, lush flavour of peach liqueur pairs wonderfully with fruit tarts, panna cotta, and ice cream. A sweet-on-sweet indulgence. Surprisingly good with chocolate as well.

Matcha Liqueur + Sweets

Matcha liqueur poured over vanilla ice cream is sublime on its own. Equally excellent alongside matcha tiramisu, cheesecake, and wagashi (Japanese confections). Mixed with milk, it becomes a relaxing matcha-latte cocktail.

Sakura Liqueur + Spring Ingredients

Sakura liqueur shines with seasonal spring ingredients — sakura mochi, strawberries, and spring-vegetable salads. Served as a sparkling aperitif, it sets an elegant tone for any meal.
